Intro

LiteDoge is a free open source project derived from Bitcoin, with the goal of providing a long-term energy-efficient scrypt-based cryptocurrency. Built on the foundation of Bitcoin, Peercoin, Blackcoin. LiteDoge, innovations such as proof-of-stake and scrypt help further advance the field of crypto-currency along with along with real world use and application of Proof of work and Proof of Stake. With a 64 second block time and difficulty retarget every block helping to make the blockchain fast and secure along with Proof of Stake 2.0. Real world use will be a software and web ecosystem with tipping bots and in game use, along with teaching how to use money in a fun way.
